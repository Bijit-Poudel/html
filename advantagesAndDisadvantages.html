Advantages And Disadvantages Of Internet

The internet has many advantages including the disadvantages too. The advantages are :

  1. Access to Information
  2. Communication
  3. Education and E-learning
  4. E-commerce and Business
  5. Entertainment
  6. Convenience and Efficiency
  7. Collaboration and Networking
  8. Global Connectivity
  9. Job Opportunities
  10. Innovation and Technology Advancement

As mentioned internet not only has advantages but it also comes with many disadvantages with some mentioned as :

  1. Cybersecurity Threats
  2. Misinformation and Fake News
  3. Addiction and Distraction
  4. Privacy Concerns
  5. Cyberbullying and Online Harassment
  6. Dependency on Technology
  7. Online Scams and Fraud
  8. Impact on Social Interaction
  9. Eye Problems
  10. Loss of Traditional Skills
