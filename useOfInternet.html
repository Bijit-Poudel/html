Uses of the Internet

The Internet is used for a wide variety of purposes, including:

  • Communication: People use the Internet to communicate with each other via email, instant messaging, and social media.
  • Information access: People use the Internet to access information from a variety of sources, including websites, news services, and online databases.
  • Commerce: People use the Internet to shop online, pay bills, and transfer money.
  • Entertainment: People use the Internet to watch videos, listen to music, and play games.
  • Education: People use the Internet to take online courses, learn new skills, and research topics of interest.
